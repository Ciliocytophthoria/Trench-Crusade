	# Copenhagen Trench Crusade #


1. We aim to match warbands that have played an equal number of games, but you are free to play with unequal warbands. In order to catch up you can either make a warband using the latecomers rules or find opponents that are equally behind and catch up that way, if the current round needs to be updated, send a message in the signal group.

2. We plan to host events with several tables once every or second month and you are free to meet up individually.

3. This campaign is expectet to last 12 games, and will follow the rules in the newest edition of the rulebook as closely as possible. Without any homebrewed rules. Afterwards, we plan to organise another campaign, following the rules from the carcass front expansion. Point being, that we aim for this campaign to last for a long time.

4. And lastly, we wholeheartedly aim for this campaign to be entertaining. You are perfectly free to both explore and optimise your warband within the limits of the ruleset, but this is also a measuring and dice game, with favorable and unfavorable matchups. Randomness means that better players with better warbands will lose to worse. We urge you to lose and win gracefully, and have fun in the process. "Be strict with yourself but lenient with others".
